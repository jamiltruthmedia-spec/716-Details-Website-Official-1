Interior Detailing FAQ

How long does interior detailing take?

A complete interior detailing typically takes 3-5 hours depending on your vehicle's size and condition. Larger vehicles like SUVs and trucks may take longer. We never rush the process to ensure every surface is thoroughly cleaned and protected to perfection.

Will interior detailing remove pet hair?

Yes! We specialize in thorough pet hair removal using professional-grade tools and techniques. Our deep vacuuming process combined with specialized brushes effectively removes embedded pet hair from carpets, seats, and even hard-to-reach areas.

Can you remove stains from my car's interior?

Most stains can be removed with our professional-grade cleaning products and steam extraction equipment. We've successfully removed coffee, soda, food, ink, and many other types of stains. However, some old or set-in stains may only lighten rather than disappear completely.

Is interior detailing safe for leather seats?

Absolutely! We use pH-balanced, leather-specific products that clean without damaging or drying out your leather. Our conditioning treatment replenishes essential oils and provides UV protection to prevent cracking and fading, keeping your leather supple for years.

Can you remove cigarette smoke odor?

Yes, we can eliminate cigarette smoke odor using a combination of deep cleaning, ozone treatment, and odor elimination products. The process may require multiple treatments for heavy smoke damage, but we can restore your vehicle's interior to a fresh, smoke-free condition.

What's included in interior detailing?

Our interior detailing includes deep vacuuming of all surfaces, steam cleaning of carpets and upholstery, leather cleaning and conditioning, dashboard and console detailing, door panel cleaning, trim restoration, window cleaning, and air vent detailing. We clean every surface your hands and eyes touch.

How often should I get interior detailing?

We recommend professional interior detailing every 3-6 months for optimal maintenance. However, if you have pets, children, or frequently transport passengers, quarterly detailing will keep your interior in pristine condition. Regular detailing also helps maintain your vehicle's resale value.

Do you offer mobile interior detailing?

Yes! We offer convenient mobile detailing services throughout Lewiston, Clarence, Williamsville, and all of Niagara County. We bring our professional equipment and expertise directly to your home or office for ultimate convenience.

Can you restore faded or damaged interior trim?

Yes, we can restore most faded or damaged interior trim pieces. Our restoration process includes specialized cleaning, conditioning, and protective treatments that can bring back the original appearance of plastic, vinyl, and rubber trim components.

Is interior detailing worth it for older vehicles?

Definitely! Interior detailing can dramatically transform an older vehicle's appearance and feel. It removes years of built-up dirt and grime, restores faded surfaces, eliminates odors, and can significantly increase your vehicle's resale value. Many customers are amazed at how "new" their older car feels after professional detailing.
