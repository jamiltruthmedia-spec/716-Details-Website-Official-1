Common Questions

FAQ

How much does ceramic coating cost?
Our ceramic coating packages start at $1,200 for a Gloss Enhancement + Ceramic on a sedan, $1,800 for a 1-Step + Ceramic, and $2,500 for a 2-Step + Ceramic. SUVs, trucks, and XL vehicles are priced accordingly. Call us for an exact quote on your vehicle.
How long does ceramic coating take?
Depending on the package, ceramic coating typically takes 1–3 days. Paint correction packages require additional time to properly prep the surface before coating is applied. We'll give you a specific timeline when you book.
Does my car need paint correction first?
It depends on the condition of your paint. Ceramic coating locks in whatever is underneath it — so if there are swirl marks or scratches, they'll be sealed in too. We recommend at least a gloss enhancement polish before any coating application for best results.
Is ceramic coating worth it in WNY winters?
Absolutely — Buffalo winters are one of the best reasons to get ceramic coating. Road salt and brine are highly corrosive to clear coat. The ceramic layer acts as a chemical barrier, preventing salt from bonding to your paint and making post-winter cleanup far easier.
How do I maintain a ceramic coated car?
Maintenance is simple — hand wash only using a pH-neutral soap, avoid automatic car washes with brushes, and dry with a clean microfiber. We walk you through the full aftercare process before you leave our shop, and we're always available if you have questions.
