Common Questions

FAQ

How much does window tint cost?
Full window tint starts at $300 for a coupe with carbon film, and $400 with ceramic. SUVs and trucks run $360–$480, XL vehicles $400–$520. Windshield tint starts at $140 (carbon) or $190 (ceramic). Visor strips start at $60. Call or submit a quote request for exact pricing on your vehicle.
How long does a window tint install take?
Most full tint installs take 2–4 hours depending on your vehicle. Partial services like windshield or visor tint are faster. We’ll give you an accurate time estimate when you book.
What’s the difference between Carbon and Ceramic film?
Both are serious upgrades over cheap dyed film. Carbon (HITEK Carbon IR) delivers strong heat rejection and a clean dark look at a fair price. Ceramic (HITEK Ceramic Black) goes further — it uses advanced infrared blocking technology for significantly better heat performance, crystal-clear clarity, and zero signal interference. If your budget allows, ceramic is the upgrade worth making.
How long does tint take to cure?
Tint typically cures in 3–7 days in warm weather. In cold weather or WNY winters, it can take up to 2 weeks. During the curing period, keep your windows up. Some initial haze or small water pockets are completely normal — they clear on their own as the film cures.
Is window tint legal in New York State?
New York has specific tint regulations for side windows, rear windows, and windshields. We’re familiar with NYS requirements and can help guide you toward compliant shade options. We recommend confirming your desired shade against current laws — we’re happy to walk you through what works for your vehicle.
