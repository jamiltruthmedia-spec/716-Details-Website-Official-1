Common Questions
FAQ
How long does a detail take? +
Interior details typically take 2–4 hours depending on condition. Full details (interior + exterior) can take 3–6 hours. Heavily soiled vehicles may require additional time for proper restoration. We’ll provide an accurate time estimate with your quote.
What’s the difference between Level 1 and Level 2? +

Level 1 is a thorough deep clean — ideal for well-maintained vehicles.

Level 2 is a full interior restoration designed for vehicles with heavy buildup, stains, or odors.

Most first-time clients benefit from starting with Level 2 to fully reset the interior before moving into maintenance.

What’s included in the ceramic spray? +
All exterior services include a 3–6 month ceramic sealant that provides hydrophobic protection, UV resistance, and enhanced gloss. It helps protect your paint and keeps your vehicle cleaner longer between washes.
Is detailing worth it in WNY winters? +
Absolutely. Salt, slush, and road grime can quickly damage both your interior and exterior. Regular detailing removes these contaminants and helps protect your vehicle throughout the winter months.
How do I maintain results after a detail? +
We recommend regular hand washes (avoiding automatic car washes), light interior upkeep with microfiber towels, and booking a maintenance detail every 60 days to keep your vehicle in top condition.
