## üìû HOW TO QUOTE PRICES

1. **Always ask vehicle type first:** "What type of vehicle do you have?"
2. **Ask about condition:** "Is it in good condition or does it need extra attention?"
3. **Provide range:** "For a [vehicle type] in [condition], that service runs $X-$Y"
4. **Explain value:** "That includes [list key features]"
5. **Offer free quote:** "I can give you an exact quote if you'd like"

**Example:**
"For a standard sedan full detail, we're looking at $290-$320, which includes complete interior deep cleaning, exterior hand wash, clay bar, wax, and about 3-4 hours of work. If your vehicle is larger or needs extra attention, it may be closer to $350. Would you like me to give you an exact quote?"
