HOW TO GIVE CUSTOMER A PRICE

Step 1: Ask what type of vehicle they have
Step 2: Ask about condition (good or needs extra work)
Step 3: Give them the price range
Step 4: Explain what is included
Step 5: Offer exact quote if needed

Example: "For a standard sedan full detail, the price is $290 to $320. This includes complete inside deep cleaning, outside hand wash, clay bar, wax, and about 3 to 4 hours of work. If your vehicle is larger or needs extra attention, it may be closer to $350."
