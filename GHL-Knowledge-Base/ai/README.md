# AI-Optimized Knowledge Base Files

This folder contains files specifically formatted for AI understanding and processing.

## Files in This Folder

### 1. Company-Overview-for-AI-Understanding.docx
- **Purpose:** Company information in simple, AI-friendly language
- **Format:** Clean DOCX (no markdown, no emojis)
- **Contents:**
  - What we do (clear service list)
  - Who we serve (customer types)
  - Where we serve (all cities and areas)
  - Our mission and values

- **Format:** Clean DOCX (no markdown, no emojis)

### 3. Services-and-Pricing-for-AI-Understanding.docx
- **Purpose:** Service descriptions in simple, AI-friendly language
- **Format:** Clean DOCX (no markdown, no emojis)
- **Contents:**
  - All 11 services explained in plain terms

  - "When customer asks" response templates
  - Upselling tips in simple language

### 4. FAQs-for-AI-Understanding.docx
- **Purpose:** Common questions with clean, direct answers
- **Format:** Clean DOCX (no markdown, no emojis, no conversational filler)
- **Contents:**
  - 40+ frequently asked questions

- **Format:** Clean DOCX (no markdown, no emojis)

  - Special cases (luxury vehicles, fleet bookings, reschedules)

### 6. services-and-packages-for-ai-understanding.csv
- **Purpose:** Simplified pricing table for AI semantic search
- **Format:** CSV with 7 simple columns (no N/A values)
- **Contents:**

  - Window Tinting explicitly listed 6 times

## Why These Files Are Different

These files use:
- ✅ Simple, clear language (no jargon)
- ✅ Plain descriptions instead of abbreviations
- ✅ "We offer..." statements (explicit, not implied)
- ✅ "3 to 4 hours" instead of "3-4 hours"
- ✅ "Mobile or Shop" instead of "Mobile or In-Shop"
- ✅ No N/A values in CSV
- ✅ Fewer columns with more descriptive content
