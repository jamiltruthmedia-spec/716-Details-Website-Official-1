CERAMIC COATING

Question: What is ceramic coating and how long does it last?

Answer: Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds with your paint creating a protective layer. It lasts 3 to 8 years versus wax lasting 2 to 3 months. It has 10H hardness that protects against scratches. It is hydrophobic so water beads off. It provides UV protection to prevent fading. It makes washing easier. It provides enhanced gloss. Our packages start at $705 depending on vehicle size and protection level desired.

---

Question: Can I get ceramic coating on any vehicle?

Answer: Yes. Ceramic coating works on cars, trucks, SUVs, boats, and RVs. For best results we recommend paint correction first if your vehicle has swirls or scratches.

---

Question: How much does ceramic coating cost?

Answer: Ceramic coating packages range from $705 to $1255. Price depends on vehicle size (sedan versus SUV versus truck), protection level (3 to 5 year versus 8 year warranty), and current paint condition. Most sedans start around $705.

---

Question: Is ceramic coating worth it?

Answer: If you plan to keep your vehicle for several years then yes. Wax costs $50 to $100 every 2 to 3 months which equals $200 to $600 per year. Ceramic coating lasts 3 to 8 years for $705 to $1255 one time. Ceramic coating provides better protection than wax. It maintains resale value. It makes maintenance easier. For luxury vehicles especially it is excellent investment protection.
